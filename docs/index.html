awesome-tf2

A curated list of awesome Team Fortress 2 resources, tools and more. To add or update a TF2 resource or site, please submit a pull request. Check the contribution guidelines for more info.

This page was created using the tf2-ui-kit by GingerBunny.

Annual Charity Events

  • Blapature Co. - EU-based charity livestream hosted by a group of TF2 YouTubers.
  • hugs.TF - UK-based charity livestream event benefiting Special Effect.
  • TFConnect - Community ran charity event focused on Mann vs. Machines, benefiting Special Effect.
  • Tip of the Hats - Charity livestream benefiting One Step Camp. Ran from 2013-2021.

Community Sites

  • BalanceMod.TF - Introduces new features and re-balances all of the unbalanced weapons in the game.
  • Creators.TF - TF2 community celebration project that takes the initiative to provide new content.
  • Essentials.TF - Competitive and community news aggregator and host of monthly NA/EU tournaments.
  • FuckYeahTF2 - Tumblr for everything and anything TF2-related, namely artwork showcases.
  • Jump.TF - The TF2 Jumping community.
  • Jump Academy - Community of jumpers from all around the world who share the same passion for rocket jumping.
  • KritzKast - THE Team Fortress 2 Podcast, hosted every Saturday by Agro.
  • Potato.TF - Community made Mann vs. Machine tours. Play and level up to earn in-game medals.
  • Teamwork.TF - Community Quickplay, Jarate Pages, news and stats about Team Fortress 2.
  • r/TF2 - Subreddit dedicated to any and all Team Fortress 2 content; news, jokes and discussions.

Competitive

  • asiafortress - Asian community website, hosting Team Fortress 2 6v6 events for the Asia region.
  • ETF2L - Free-to-play European league, featuring Highlander, 6v6, occasional tournaments and the Nations Cup.
  • Newbie.TF - Play your first games of competitive TF2 with other new players, led by an experienced coach.
  • ozfortress - Australian community 6v6 league.
  • RGL - World-wide Prolander (7v7) and Highlander league.,stats and more!
  • TeamFortress TV - Competitive TF2 news, events, discussions, streams, stats and more!
  • TF2Center - TF2Center was created to help bridge the gap between public and competitive play for TF2.
  • UGC League - Worldwide league, primarily catering to North American Highlander and 6v6.
